## Defines leadership as:
- The ability to build an maintain a team that can outperform the competition
## Good Leaders have:
- Integrity
- Good Judgment
- Competence
- Vision
## Bad Leaders are:
- Arrogant
- Manipulative
- Emotionally Volatile
- Micromanaging
- Passive Aggressive
- Distrustful of Others
